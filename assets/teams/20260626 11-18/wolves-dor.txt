Clear Description
Product Owner Responsibility: Ensure descriptions are clearly articulated and understandable to all.
Answer the 4 Questions:
- What? = What are we building, what is the context (bigger picture), what is the work increment?
- Why? = What is the business value, what is the impact?
- Priority? = What is most important and why, are there time constraints?
- Risks? = Dependencies, other teams, potential blockers, data sources - everything the team cannot resolve by itself
Comments: Have holistic understanding of project and product - share this knowledge with the team. Provide the contact point to other teams. Potential blockers should be made explicit by Product Owner. Link to all dependent tickets in Jira.

Verifiable Acceptance Criteria
Product Owner Responsibility: Define Measurable Acceptance Criteria, meaning conditions for accepting the product created by the team. Ensures they are clearly articulated and understandable to all.
Make them SMART: Specific - Clear and unambiguous, Measurable - Quantifiable with defined metrics, Achievable - Realistic given constraints.
If numbers appear: Explain where they come from (data source, analysis). Identify whose requirement it is (business, technical, regulatory).
Separate into: Outputs = What the team controls and delivers, Outcomes = What the team doesn't control but strives to achieve through its product and work.

Collaborative Progress
Team Values: We trust each other. We don't avoid conversations - we communicate immediately in two directions. Business people and developers work together daily throughout the project.
Backlog Refinement: Weekly discussion to gain clarity and alignment on backlog. Brainstorm solutions. Does not require full team attendance.
