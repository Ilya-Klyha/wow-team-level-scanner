Team DoR

Criterion: Task subject
Description: Task subject is descriptive and unique.

Criterion: User Story/Requirement Clarity
Description: Clear user story context understandable for team members. Task reporter may suggest solutions in some occasions.

Criterion: Acceptance Criteria
Description: Specific and testable acceptance criteria.

Criterion: Estimates Provided
Description: Team has to provide realistic estimate of ticket. While estimation teams takes into consideration: complexity, Uncertainty, Effort.

Criterion: Dependencies
Description: Requirements from external teams/repo owners regarding development when needed. All dependencies are set in Jira ticket.
