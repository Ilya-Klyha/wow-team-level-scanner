Company Definition of Ready (DoR) Standard
Source: https://adgear.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AGILE/pages/21735179128/Definition+of+Ready+DOR
Fetched: 2026-06-15

The DoR answers: Can we start this work with confidence?
Common readiness criteria:
- Clearly defined acceptance criteria
- Dependencies identified and addressed
- Scope and priority established

INVEST criteria:
- Independent, Negotiable, Valuable, Estimable, Small, Testable
