DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) - STORY/TASK
=====================================

User Story/Requirement Clarity
The user story or requirement is clearly articulated, concise, and understandable to all team members.
Context and requirements should be well defined.
If it possible, in tasks add helpful technical aspects or details.

Contact persons
All external tasks should contain contact information for stakeholders or other important persons.

Acceptance Criteria
Specific, testable acceptance criteria are provided for the user story, outlining what constitutes successful completion.

Monitoring and Alerting Configured (if applicable)
Necessary monitoring and alerting for the new feature or change have been configured.

Estimates Provided
The development team has provided an estimate for the work.

Dependencies
Highlighted dependencies with other teams, milestones and deadlines.
