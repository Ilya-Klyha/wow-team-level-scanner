DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) - STORY/TASK
=====================================

User Story/Requirement Clarity
Clear user story context understandable for team members. Task reporter may suggest solutions in some occasions.

Acceptance Criteria and Measurement Defined
Specific, testable acceptance criteria are provided for the user story, outlining what constitutes successful completion.

Monitoring and Alerting Configured (if applicable)
Necessary monitoring and alerting for the new feature or change have been configured (if needed).

Estimates Provided
Team has to provide realistic estimate of ticket.
While estimation teams takes into conisderation:
- complexity
- Uncertainty
- Effort

Dependencies
UI Mocs, Requirements from external teams/repo owner regarding development when needed. KT sessions.
