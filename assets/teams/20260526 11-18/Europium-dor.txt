DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) - STORY/TASK
=====================================

A backlog item is Ready when

Clear problem statement exists. (for story and for bug reproduction  path/steps) - team agree what should be defined in story/task before planning

Business value is explained (if needed - for story)

Acceptance criteria are defined and testable.

UX design (if applicable) is finalized and available in Figma (main overview will be delivered in Jira before planning)

Dependencies are identified  (if needed - tech spec should be approved before dev starts).

Scope is small enough to fit within a sprint

Open questions are resolved

Not Ready if:

Requirements are vague.

Design is incomplete.

Critical dependency is unknown.

Acceptance criteria are missing.

No work starts without meeting DoR (except production incidents)
