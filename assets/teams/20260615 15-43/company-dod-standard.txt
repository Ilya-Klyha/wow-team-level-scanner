Company Definition of Done (DoD) Standard
Source: https://adgear.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/AGILE/pages/21735212005/Definition+of+Done+DoD
Fetched: 2026-06-15

The DoD answers: Can we close this work with confidence?
Key principles:
- Consistent bar for completeness
- Builds trust inside and outside the team
- Reduces hidden work and rework
- Multi-level: task, story, feature/release
- Visible, achievable, evolutionary
