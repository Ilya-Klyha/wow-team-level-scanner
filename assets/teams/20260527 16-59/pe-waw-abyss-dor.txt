=== DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) – STORY / TASK ===

The "Definition of Ready" ensures that a software development ticket is sufficiently prepared and understood by the development team before it is pulled into a sprint or iteration. A ticket meeting the DoR is ready for immediate development without requiring further clarification or investigation.

| Criterion | Description | Comments |
|-----------|-------------|----------|
| User Story/Requirement Clarity | The user story or requirement is clearly articulated, concise, and understandable to all team members. | |
| Acceptance Criteria and Measurement Defined | Specific, testable acceptance criteria are provided for the user story, outlining what constitutes successful completion. | |
| Monitoring and Alerting Configured (if applicable) | Necessary monitoring and alerting for the new feature or change have been configured. | Configuration: what and where should be configured? Metrics: how do we measure story/task? |
| Estimates Provided | The development team has provided an estimate for the work, or the ticket has been broken down to a size that allows for reasonable estimation. | Estimations are based on the whole team experience. Story Estimation - Cheat Sheet - Google Sheets |
| Dependencies | Highlighted dependencies with other teams or milestones. | All blocking tasks are resolved. |
