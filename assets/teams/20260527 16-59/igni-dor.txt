=== DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) - STORY/TASK ===

| Criterion | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| User Story/Requirement Clarity | The user story or requirement is clearly articulated, concise, and understandable to all team members. Context and requirements should be well defined. If it possible, in tasks add helpful technical aspects or details. |
| Contact persons | All external tasks should contain contact information for stakeholders or other important persons. |
| Acceptance Criteria | Specific, testable acceptance criteria are provided for the user story, outlining what constitutes successful completion. |
| Monitoring and Alerting Configured (if applicable) | Necessary monitoring and alerting for the new feature or change have been configured. |
| Estimates Provided | The development team has provided an estimate for the work. |
| Dependencies | Highlighted dependencies with other teams, milestones and deadlines. |

Additional team information:
- Warsaw based Team
- The "Definition of Ready" ensures that a software development ticket is sufficiently prepared and understood by the development team before it is pulled into a sprint or iteration.
- A ticket meeting the DoR is ready for immediate development without requiring further clarification or investigation.
