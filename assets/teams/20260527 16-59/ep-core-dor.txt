Additional team information:
- Mission Statement: The Engineering Platform (EP) Core team builds, maintains, and evolves a self-service, secure, and observable platform that enables all engineering teams at Samsung Ads & future also in SBT to deploy, operate, and scale their applications efficiently.
- Core Values:
  * Developer Experience First: Every decision is evaluated through the lens of developer productivity
  * Security is Non-Negotiable: Never compromise on security; controls are built-in, not bolted on
  * Operational Excellence: Run our own platform services with the same rigor we expect from customers
  * Collaboration Over Silos: Close collaboration with all engineering teams to understand needs and build solutions together
  * Continuous Improvement: Learn from failures, and iterate rapidly
