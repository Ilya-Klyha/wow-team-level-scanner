TEAM DoR
================================================================================

- The user story and/or requirement is clearly articulated, concise, and
  understandable to all team members. Concrete and measurable.

- We have clear & testable Acceptance Criteria.

- All necessary documents are provided if task requires such like TechSpecs.
  Task was estimated by the team. Story points are assigned.

- All dependencies of tasks and blockers are defined and resolved (inside and
  outside).

- Label Lifeguard is added to ticket (when it is needed).

Team Charter Mission:
The Engineering Platform (EP) Core team builds, maintains, and evolves a self-
service, secure, and observable platform that enables all engineering teams at
Samsung Ads & future also in SBT to deploy, operate, and scale their
applications efficiently. We provide the foundational infrastructure and
tooling that powers our production workloads.

Core Values:
1. Developer Experience First
2. Security is Non-Negotiable
3. Operational Excellence
4. Collaboration Over Silos
5. Continuous Improvement
