=== DEFINITION OF READY (DoR) - STORY/TASK ===

- We have clear & testable Acceptance Criteria.
- All necessary documents are provided if task requires such like TechSpecs.
- Task was estimated by the team. Story points are assigned.
- All dependencies of tasks and blockers are defined and resolved (inside and outside).
- Label Lifeguard is added to ticket (when it is needed).
