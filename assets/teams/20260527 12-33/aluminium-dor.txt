Criterion | Description
---------|------------

Contact persons | All external tasks should contain contact information for stakeholders or other important persons.
Acceptance Criteria | Specific, testable acceptance criteria are provided for the user story, outlining what constitutes successful completion.
Monitoring and Alerting Configured (if applicable) | Necessary monitoring and alerting for the new feature or change have been configured.
Estimates Provided | The development team has provided an estimate for the work.
Dependencies | Highlighted dependencies with other teams, milestones and deadlines.
