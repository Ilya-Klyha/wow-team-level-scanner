Team SRPOL SRE - DoR

Note: The SRE team page does not contain explicit DoR/DoD definitions. The page primarily references team ceremony schedules and working agreements.

Working Agreement highlights:
- Slack is main way of communication
- Slack channels: team members can be found in #team-sre and #sre
- Microsoft Teams is used for ceremonies that requires external guests (non-team)
- If there is another scrum ceremony the same day, we skip daily
- No meetings on Fridays (unless there is an emergency)
- No touching production on Fridays or day before any holiday
- Scrum Master changes every sprint
- Each team member has to inform other team members before or during Sprint Planning about Annual Leaves or other Leaves which might affect next Sprint
