===== Definition of Ready - STORY/TASK =====

Bigos DoR and DoD for Story/Task

Reference: https://adgear.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/ENG/pages/22175482213

[NOTE: The main team page references a separate page for DoR and DoD details. The page indicates that Bigos operates using Scrumbut framework with 2-week sprints. Stories should be vertical slices meeting INVEST characteristics. Estimations are done in story points using pseudo-Fibonacci scale through Planning Poker or Affinity Mapping.]

Team practices:
- User stories preferred over PBI (Product Backlog Item) form
- Stories should be vertical slices meeting INVEST characteristics
- Estimating stories in story points using a pseudo-Fibonacci scale by the development team playing Planning Poker or Affinity Mapping
